How To Make Mentai Rice!

When you eat something that cooked by
yourself, the happiness is priceless.

Ingredients:

    1 Salmon Fillet

    1 Pinch of salt and pepper

    1 tbs of butter

    1 serving of cook rice

    1 sheet of seaweed

    1 tbs of soy sauce

    seaweed powder

Ingredients For The Sauce:

    3 tbs of mayonaise

    1 tbs of chili sauce

    1 pinch of salt and sugar

    seaweed powder for taste

Directions:

    first, Prepare a bowl, pour all the ingredients for the sauce into it. Mix well.

    then, cut salmon thinly ( to make cooking time faster ), and add a liltle bit of salt, pepper and soy sauce.

    after that add the butter into the pan and wait until it's hot

    then, add the salmon, make sure they cooked eavenly and let them rest

    after the sauce and salmon are ready put your rice into a container and add 1 sheet of seaweed on the top

    arrange the salmon on the rice, pour the mentai sauce over the salmon until it is covers the entire surface

    last, add nori powder on top as garnish.
